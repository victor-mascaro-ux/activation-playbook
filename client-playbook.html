Orientation

How to Use This Playbook

This playbook covers existing clients only. Knomee here isn't a marketing funnel — it's a relationship instrument: a simple, respectful way to ask “what's changed?” and actually use the answer. Every client falls into one of two types.

🌱

Engaged

Responsive, open to guidance. Deepen the relationship — grow share of wallet.

⏳

Disengaged

Less responsive, declining meetings. Reopen communication — retain the assets.

This playbook is designed to be walked through together. Your Knomee customer success contact will schedule a 45-minute working session to:

  • 15 min — choose your first play and customize the copy

  • 10 min — CRM sync, tracking, and success metrics

The Product

What Your Client Experiences

Knomee is a 10-minute guided check-in your clients complete before a review — or any time life shifts. It helps them articulate their current goals, priorities, and top money questions, so every conversation starts with what matters now, not what mattered at onboarding.

1

The client feels heard

They get a personal summary — and reviews stop feeling like a portfolio report.

2

The advisor sees what changed

Knomee surfaces shifts in priorities and life events before the meeting.

3

The firm keeps the relationship

Knomee catches drift early and opens doors to the broader household.

Why This Matters Commercially

Client use of Knomee drives retention, annual-review participation, household penetration, and share of wallet — different economics than prospect acquisition, and often a faster payoff. A single retained at-risk household typically covers the program.

Before You Launch

The 5-Step Setup

Every campaign follows the same five setup steps. Work through them to prepare for your walkthrough session; your CS contact will help you complete each one.

1
Targeting

Segment your book: engaged (upcoming reviews) vs. disengaged (6+ months quiet).

2
Cadence

Map the plays to your review calendar (see the cadence table in Engaged).

3
Copy

Customize samples with the advisor's name and voice — 1:1 messages, not blasts.

4
Link

Use your client invite URL from the Knomee dashboard (different from the prospect link).

5
CRM

Sync completions to HubSpot / Redtail so advisors see responses before every meeting.

The Handoff: Disengaged → Engaged

The moment a disengaged client completes Knomee, treat them as Engaged: the advisor reviews responses within 48 hours, sends the thank-you, and books the meeting. A re-engagement completion that sits unanswered does more harm than no campaign at all.

Optimize

Test Before You Launch

Even 1:1 client outreach benefits from testing. Knomee supports UTM tags so you can see which message and timing drives completions — not just opens.

1
Copy your client invite URL from the Knomee Advisor dashboard (different from the prospect URL).
2
Tag it with UTM parameters. knomeeurl.com/?utm_source=email&utm_campaign=midyear_pulse&utm_content=option1
3
Read Knomee's traffic report, then standardize on the winning message across the book.

The 10% Rule

Pilot each play with a handful of advisors or 10% of the target segment first. Once completion and response rates prove out, roll it out book-wide.

Three Steps to Your First Campaign
  1. Pick your lead type. Where are most of the prospects in your pipeline today? Start with that section — you don't need to run all three at once.

What Is Knomee — and Why It Works in Marketing

Knomee is a 10-minute guided experience your prospects complete on their own, before they ever sit down with an advisor. Instead of asking about assets, it helps them get clear on their goals, values, and the three money questions that matter most to them.

When a prospect completes Knomee, three things happen:
  1. The prospect gets immediate value — a personal summary of what's driving their financial decisions, and their top 3 money questions. No cost, no obligation, no financial data required.
  2. Your firm gets a signal — a 0–100 Advisor Readiness Score that tells you who is high-intent and worth advisor time right now.
  3. Your advisor gets a playbook — an AI-powered Prospect Playbook with conversation starters, the words to use (and avoid), and answers to the prospect's top money questions.
Why Marketers Love This
Every campaign needs a call to action — and “book a meeting” is a big ask for someone who barely knows you. Knomee gives you a low-friction, high-value CTA for every channel: “spend 10 minutes getting clear on what your money is for.” It converts attention into identified, qualified prospects — without feeling like a pitch.
Before You Launch: The 5-Step Setup
  1. Targeting — identify the list of prospects for this campaign (and which lead type they are).
  2. Channel — choose email, ads, events, direct mail, or a mix, using the channel guide in each section.
  3. Copy — customize the sample copy with your firm name, advisor name, and voice.
  4. Landing page — set up or link to your firm-branded Knomee landing page (see “Your Landing Page”).
  5. CRM — sync Knomee to your CRM (HubSpot, Redtail) so completions and scores flow to advisors automatically.

COLD LEAD
They don't know you yet. Don't ask for a meeting — ask for ten minutes of curiosity.
Who this is
Purchased or rented lists, paid social audiences, employer or association partnerships, cold referrals.
They're thinking
“I don't know this advisor. I'm not looking for a new one right now — and I get outreach like this all the time.”
Your goal
Generate awareness. Earn a first response. Convert an unknown name into a self-identified warm prospect.
The one rule
Every cold touchpoint routes to one low-friction action — completing Knomee. Never ask a cold lead for a meeting.
Your Campaign, Step by Step
  1. Cold email — lead with curiosity, not a pitch.
  2. Paid social / display ad — route clicks to your Knomee landing page, not your homepage.
  3. Direct mail with QR code — a physical touchpoint into a digital experience.
  4. Partner or association co-branded invite — borrow trust from an employer or group your prospect already belongs to.

The Handoff: Cold → Warm
Cold outreach converts at a lower rate than warm or hot — that's expected. The win isn't a meeting; it's a completion. The moment someone completes Knomee, they've moved themselves to Warm. HOT LEAD
They've agreed to meet — but they're not convinced yet. Make the first meeting feel like the tenth.
Who this is
Referrals, direct inquiries, prospects with a meeting on the calendar — from email, your website, or a current client.
They're thinking
“I've requested information or agreed to meet, but I'm not convinced this advisor is the one yet.”
Your goal
Build trust before the meeting, differentiate your firm, and accelerate the close.
The one rule
This is a 1:1 channel. Every message comes from the advisor, personally — not from marketing.
Your Campaign, Step by Step
  1. Meeting confirmation email — invite the prospect to complete Knomee before you meet.
  2. Reminder, 2 days before — one version if they haven't completed Knomee, another if they have.
  3. Meeting follow-up — keep the conversation open if the prospect hasn't signed yet.

Your Landing Page

Every channel in this playbook points to one destination: your firm-branded Knomee landing page. Place it prominently on your website and use it as the CTA target for emails, ads, QR codes, and social posts. It's fully brandable for your firm.

Sample Landing Page Copy

Headline: “Wealth is about experiencing life fully. Take a few minutes to get clear on what matters most to you.”

What you'll get: Discover what matters most · See your personal insights · Get your 3 most important money questions

Button: “Discover Financial Joy” · Est. time: 8 minutes

Five Rules for the Page
  1. Lead with the person, not the product. “Get to know your money, so you can build the life you want” reads very differently than “Take our financial assessment.” Prospects should never feel this is a pitch to buy something.
  2. Reduce friction. State the time commitment up front (8–10 minutes), make clear it's free with no obligation to meet anyone, and show what they get immediately.
  3. One primary CTA. One path, no competing buttons.
  4. Show your firm. Your logo, brand colors, and — where possible — a real advisor photo. Familiarity is a trust signal.
  5. Offer a second path for the not-ready. A lighter action — newsletter signup or your video library — so a cold visit isn't wasted if they won't commit ten minutes today.

Test Before You Launch

Don't guess which message will land — test it. Knomee supports UTM tags so you can see exactly which channel, campaign, and message drives completions (not just clicks).

  1. Copy your prospect invite URL from the Knomee Advisor dashboard.
  2. Tag it with UTM parameters for source, campaign, and message. Example: knomeeurl.com/?utm_source=email&utm_campaign=cold_email&utm_content=option1
  3. Read Knomee's traffic report to find your most effective channel and message — then adapt your plan.
The 10% Rule
Send each new campaign to 10% of your list first, with 2–3 message variants. Once you know what works, launch the winner to the rest. This playbook's Option 1 / Option 2 samples are built to be your first A/B test.

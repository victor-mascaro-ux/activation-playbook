Orientation

How to Use This Playbook

This playbook covers prospects only — people who are not yet clients. Every prospect is one of three lead types, each with its own section and ready-to-use materials.

❄️

Cold

Doesn't know your firm yet. Spark curiosity — earn a first response.

🌤️

Warm

Knows you, but isn't ready. Deliver value early — earn the meeting.

🔥

Hot

Agreed to meet, not convinced. Build trust — close the relationship.

Your Walkthrough Session

This playbook is designed to be walked through together. Your Knomee customer success contact will schedule a 45-minute working session to:

  • Pick your priority lead type and target list
  • Choose channels and customize the sample copy
  • Set up your landing page and tracking links
  • Agree on success metrics

You'll leave with your first campaign ready to send.

The Product

What Your Prospect Experiences

Knomee is a 10-minute guided experience your prospects complete on their own, before they ever sit down with an advisor.

1

The prospect gets value

A personal summary of what's driving their decisions and their top 3 money questions.

2

Your firm gets a signal

A 0–100 Advisor Readiness Score that tells you who is high-intent right now.

3

Your advisor gets a playbook

Conversation starters, words to use (and avoid), and answers to their top questions.

One Call to Action

Every campaign uses the same call to action — the 10-minute Knomee experience — never “book a meeting.” Keep it consistent across every channel and lead type, so prospects always know the next step.

❄️ Cold Lead

Cold

They don't know you yet. Don't ask for a meeting — ask for ten minutes of curiosity.

Who this is
Purchased or rented lists, paid social audiences, employer or association partnerships, cold referrals.
They're thinking
“I don't know this advisor. I'm not looking for a new one right now — and I get outreach like this all the time.”
Your goal
Generate awareness. Earn a first response. Convert an unknown name into a self-identified warm prospect.
The one rule
Every cold touchpoint routes to one low-friction action — completing Knomee. Never ask a cold lead for a meeting.

🔥 Hot Lead

Hot

They've agreed to meet — but they're not convinced yet. Make the first meeting feel like the tenth.

Who this is
Referrals, direct inquiries, prospects with a meeting on the calendar — from email, your website, or a current client.
They're thinking
“I've requested information or agreed to meet, but I'm not convinced this advisor is the one yet.”
Your goal
Build trust before the meeting, differentiate your firm, and accelerate the close.
The one rule
This is a 1:1 channel. Every message comes from the advisor, personally — not from marketing.

Invite the prospect to complete Knomee before you meet.

Four Rules for the Page

1
Lead with the person, not the product.

Prospects should never feel this is a pitch to buy something.

2
Reduce friction.

State the time commitment up front, make clear it's free, and show what they get immediately.

3
One primary CTA.

One path, no competing buttons.

4
Show your firm.

Your logo, brand colors, and a real advisor photo. Familiarity is a trust signal.

Optimize

Test Before You Launch

Don't guess which message will land — test it. Knomee supports UTM tags so you can see exactly which channel, campaign, and message drives completions (not just clicks).

1
Copy your prospect invite URL from the Knomee Advisor dashboard.
2
Tag it with UTM parameters. knomeeurl.com/?utm_source=email&utm_campaign=cold_email&utm_content=option1
3
Read Knomee's traffic report to find your most effective channel and message — then adapt.

The 10% Rule

Send each new campaign to 10% of your list first, with 2–3 message variants. Once you know what works, launch the winner to the rest. This playbook's Option 1 / Option 2 samples are built to be your first A/B test.

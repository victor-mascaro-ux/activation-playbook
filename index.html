What is Knomee?

Knomee is a client insight platform that drives organic growth. It helps advisors convert the right prospects faster by powering personalized conversations. With behavioral intelligence integrated directly into existing workflows, wealth firms find more efficiency, scale, engagement, and retention.

Challenges Facing Advisors

Traditional outreach relies on generalized messaging that misses the emotional context behind financial decision-making.

Without behavioral insight, advisors spend time on low-intent prospects, leading to inefficient engagement, lower conversion, and longer sales cycles.

Engaging and ensuring alignment with existing clients to drive retention can be a challenge and business is often lost during liquidity and life events.

How Knomee Solves It

Prospects are invited to a 10-minute research-backed digital experience to help them articulate their goals and top 3 money questions before the conversation begins.

Responses generate a 0–100 Advisor Readiness Score, helping firms identify high-intent prospects, prioritize follow-up, and focus advisor time most effectively.

The advisor receives an AI-powered Prospect Playbook with specific language to use, words to avoid, conversation starters, and answers to the prospect's three most important money questions.

Knomee's Impact
01

Gives prospects meaningful value and builds trust before asking for financial data.

02

Delivers a differentiated experience centered on the individual's goals, values, and motivations.

03

Refines language to address the prospect's needs and values.

04

Aligns ensemble teams with consistent, structured, scalable, and dynamic understanding.

05

Discovers what has changed for a household since the last conversation.

Capturing Testing Data

Knomee supports UTM tags to track source, campaign, and content with incoming traffic. Add UTM parameters to invite URLs used in ads and email communications to track where traffic comes from and test different messaging with a pilot group before a full launch.

UTM Implementation
1
Copy the Knomee client or prospect invite URL from the Advisor dashboard.📋 Dashboard
2
Append the appropriate UTM tags identifying the source, campaign, and messaging used.🏷 UTM tags
3
Use Knomee's traffic report to determine the most effective medium and messaging, then adapt your plan accordingly.📊 Reporting
